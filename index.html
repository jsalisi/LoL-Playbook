Objectives

What are objectives?

Objectives are exactly what they sound like; they are big and important. While not all objectives are required to win a match, they help you and your team progress throughout the game.

Specifically, these objectives are split into 2 categories: Structures and Jungle (a.k.a. Neutral Objectives).

Structures: Nexus, Inhibitors, and Turrets.

Jungle: Baron Nashor, Dragon, and Blue and Red Buff.

*1 Blue or Red dot represents 1 player
